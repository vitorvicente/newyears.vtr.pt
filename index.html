Short Introduction



When I first made this website, its goal was to enumerate my new-year's resolutions, but since I've watched CGP Grey's Cortex episode on new-year's themes, and after some thought, it really makes a lot more sense to have a theme for your year rather than a few specific objectives.

The Theme



I understand that "Year Of Basis" is a very vague theme, but it does have some reasoning behind it. As this year I intend on focusing on improving myself on several different areas, as to establish a good and proper basis for me to recover from what was an incredibly downing year, so that I use my next year to strive forward.
